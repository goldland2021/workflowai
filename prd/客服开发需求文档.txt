# 1. Product Overview

## Product Name

AI Employee

## Product Positioning

AI Employee is an AI front desk for service businesses.

It responds to customers instantly, captures contact information, collects business requirements, detects important business events, summarizes conversations, and only escalates to the business owner when a commercial decision is required.

The product is designed for owner-operated businesses.

Examples:

* Airport Transfer
* Plumber
* Electrician
* Cleaning Company
* Photographer
* Lawyer
* Medical Clinic
* Moving Company

---

# 2. Product Goal

The product must solve four problems.

1. Never let customers wait.

2. Never lose a potential customer.

3. Reduce repetitive customer service work.

4. Allow the owner to focus only on business decisions.

Collect information

Answer FAQ

Generate summaries

Suggest quotations

Detect business events

Capture contact information

Generate bookings

Approve discounts

Approve refunds

Promise compensation

Change prices

Accept cancellations without owner approval

Modify business rules

---

# 10. Product Principles

1. AI always responds immediately.

2. AI always captures customer contact whenever appropriate.

3. AI always summarizes instead of showing raw conversations.

4. AI detects business events instead of simply chatting.

5. AI asks the owner only when necessary.

6. Owner controls business decisions.

7. Every feature must save the owner's time.
